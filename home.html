    Arangetram Cultural Dance

    Arangetram is a pivotal event in Indian classical dance, symbolizing a dancer's debut after years of training. It celebrates their mastery and readiness to perform independently, highlighting the rich traditions of the art form. The performance is often accompanied by live music and elaborate costumes, reflecting the cultural heritage and artistic depth of the dance.
