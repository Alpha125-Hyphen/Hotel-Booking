OUR FACILITIES

The Taj Hotel chain offers a luxurious experience with an array of high-end facilities designed for comfort and convenience. Guests can enjoy elegantly furnished rooms and suites, often with stunning views and modern amenities. Dining options include a variety of venues serving exquisite cuisines, from fine dining to casual fare. The hotels feature full-service spas, state-of-the-art fitness centers, and wellness programs to ensure a rejuvenating stay. Business travelers benefit from well-equipped conference rooms, meeting spaces, and comprehensive business services. Many locations offer outdoor swimming pools with ample lounging areas, while concierge services provide personalized assistance with bookings, transportation, and local recommendations. Additionally, the hotels are well-equipped for hosting events such as weddings and banquets, and provide luxury amenities including high-quality toiletries and 24-hour room service.

Wifi

At Taj Hotels, guests can expect high-speed, reliable Wi-Fi connectivity throughout the property, ensuring seamless internet access for both business and leisure needs.

Phone

Taj Hotels provide excellent phone services to enhance guest convenience and connectivity. Each guest room is equipped with a high-quality telephone system, featuring direct dialing for local and international calls.

Bluetooth

Taj Hotels incorporate modern Bluetooth technology to enhance guest convenience and comfort. Many guest rooms are equipped with Bluetooth-enabled devices, such as speakers or alarm clocks, allowing guests to easily connect their smartphones or other devices for streaming music or making hands-free calls

Settings

Taj Hotels offer a range of thoughtfully designed settings and amenities to ensure a comfortable and luxurious stay. The settings are often characterized by elegant décor and a blend of modern and traditional styles, creating a welcoming and sophisticated atmosphere.

Camera

Taj Hotels are known for their scenic and aesthetically pleasing environments, making them ideal for photography. While the hotels generally encourage guests to capture their memorable experiences, they also adhere to specific policies to ensure privacy and comfort.

Night Mode

Taj Hotels prioritize guest comfort with thoughtful night mode settings designed to enhance relaxation and ensure a restful night's sleep. Many guest rooms are equipped with adjustable lighting options, allowing guests to set a soft, ambient light that is easy on the eyes and conducive to relaxation.
